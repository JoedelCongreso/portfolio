About Me

Hello! I'm Mary Joedel, a passionate Frontend Developer with a knack for creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from Quezon City University, where I developed a strong foundation in web development and design principles. Over the past 4 years, I have honed my skills in various frontend technologies and frameworks, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap5, and React.

I gained hands-on experience through academic projects and activities, allowing me to apply my knowledge in practical settings.

In addition to my frontend expertise, I am also knowledgeable about PHP, SQL, cybersecurity, networking, and various backend technologies. This diverse skill set allows me to have a comprehensive understanding of the full stack, enabling me to build more robust and secure applications.

One of the biggest projects I created was my Capstone project named QCEMS (Quezon City University Event Management System), which we proposed to our university. QCUEMS is an event management system that aims to enhance the experience of organizing and managing events within and outside the university. It also allows the university to handle events from other organizations.

What drives me in this field is the constant evolution of technology and the endless opportunities to learn and innovate. I am particularly interested in the areas of responsive design, accessibility, and web performance optimization.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy traveling locally, playing music, and exploring new museums around the city. These activities help me stay creative and inspired, bringing fresh perspectives to my work.
